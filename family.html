FAQ

Family trip questions

Is Japan good with kids?

Yes, but the plan should reduce transfers, avoid overpacking, and include enough simple food/rest options.

Can seniors travel comfortably?

Yes, if hotels, station access, route pace, and walking load are planned carefully.

Should families include Fuji?

It depends on season, weather expectations, travel days, and whether the family values a slower scenic pause.
